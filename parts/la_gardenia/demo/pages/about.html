La Gardenia started as food truck owned by ms. Orozso (also known as Ama for family) Due to high quality and service the succesful food truck slowly but surely grown into a little restaurant. Hard work and authentic recepies made La Gardenia the restaurant it is today

The team showes up at 3 am to get restarant open at 4 am for the early birds and hard workers from surrounding plants in Texas city. Most of the guest say "hi" to the chef personaly since most of them come there on dayily basis.
